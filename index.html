Medical Bias

Our Purpose

Implicit Bias in healthcare exists.
Thanks to recent studys, this has become a new focus of the medical community. As medical professionals begin to learn and address their biases, there are no public resources for those affected to try to get the care they deserve. BiasBreaker serves to create a community for women and BIPOC to share advice on navigating experiences with medical professionals.
